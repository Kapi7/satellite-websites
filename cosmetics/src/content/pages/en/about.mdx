We built Glow Coded because we were tired of skincare advice that was either too salesy or too vague. We wanted a place where you could learn what actually works, backed by ingredient science and months of personal testing.

## What We Believe

Good skincare is simple skincare. You don't need 12 steps or $200 serums. You need the right ingredients, in the right order, used consistently. We lean heavily into Korean beauty philosophy because it prioritizes skin health over quick fixes.

## Our Editorial Standards

Every product we recommend has been tested for at least four weeks before we write about it. We don't accept paid placements. If we link to a product, it's because we genuinely use it.

- No sponsored content or paid reviews
- All ingredient claims are cross-referenced with published research
- Product recommendations come from personal, long-term testing
- Articles are updated when new evidence or reformulations emerge
