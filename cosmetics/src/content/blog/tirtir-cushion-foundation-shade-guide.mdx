TIRTIR's Mask Fit cushion foundations have become some of the most popular base products in K-beauty, and for good reason. The shade range is unusually inclusive for a Korean brand (up to 45 shades in some lines), the formulas deliver genuine skin-like coverage, and the cushion format makes application and touch-ups effortless.

But with five different cushion lines and dozens of shades, finding your match can be overwhelming. This guide breaks down every TIRTIR cushion formula, what it does best, and how to find your shade.

## The TIRTIR Cushion Lineup

### 1. Mask Fit Red Cushion (The Bestseller)

[![TIRTIR Mask Fit Red Cushion](/images/products/tirtir-red-cushion.jpg)](https://mirai-skin.com/products/tirtir-mask-fit-red-cushion-spf40-pa-18g-45shades)

**Coverage.** Medium to full. Buildable.
**Finish.** Semi-matte, skin-like.
**SPF.** 40 PA++
**Shades.** 45 shades (the widest range).
**Best for.** Most skin types. This is the universal recommendation.

The Red Cushion is TIRTIR's flagship for a reason. It delivers that coveted "your skin but better" finish, blurring imperfections without looking cakey. The formula is lightweight despite the coverage level, and it wears well for 6 to 8 hours.

The 45-shade range is remarkable for a Korean cushion. It includes fair, light, medium, tan, and deep shades with warm, neutral, and cool undertones.

**Also available in mini** (4.5g) for testing before committing to the full size.

### 2. Mask Fit Crystal Mesh Cushion

**Coverage.** Light to medium. Natural.
**Finish.** Dewy, luminous.
**SPF.** 50+ PA++++
**Shades.** 15 shades.
**Best for.** Dry skin. People who want a glowy, "glass skin" finish.

The Crystal Mesh uses a unique mesh puff that delivers a thinner, more even layer of product. The result is a sheer, luminous finish that looks like healthy, well-hydrated skin rather than makeup. The higher SPF is a bonus.

Not ideal for oily skin; the dewy finish can look overly shiny by midday.

### 3. Mask Fit All Cover Cushion

**Coverage.** Full. The highest coverage in the lineup.
**Finish.** Matte.
**SPF.** 50+ PA++++
**Shades.** 15 shades.
**Best for.** Acne scars, hyperpigmentation, or anyone who wants maximum coverage.

This is the heavy-hitter. One layer covers redness, dark spots, and acne scarring. The matte finish controls oil well, making it the best choice for oily skin types who need serious coverage.

The trade-off is that full-coverage cushions can look heavier in person. Apply in thin layers and blend well to avoid a mask-like effect.

### 4. Mask Fit Aura Cushion

**Coverage.** Light. Sheer.
**Finish.** Natural with a soft glow.
**SPF.** 30 PA++
**Shades.** 20 shades.
**Best for.** Minimal makeup looks. "No-makeup makeup" days.

The lightest option. This is essentially a tinted moisturizer in cushion format. It evens skin tone and adds a subtle glow without any visible coverage. Perfect for weekends, casual days, or for people who dislike the feel of foundation.

### 5. Mask Fit AI Filter Cushion

**Coverage.** Medium.
**Finish.** Blurred, filtered. Looks like a beauty filter in real life.
**SPF.** 50+ PA++++
**Shades.** 15 to 35 shades (varies by version).
**Best for.** Photos, events, anyone who wants a polished, "filtered" look.

The AI Filter Cushion uses light-diffusing technology to blur pores and smooth texture visually. The effect is noticeable in photos and video. It's the most "makeup-looking" of the five cushions but achieves it through smart technology rather than heavy coverage.

## How to Find Your Shade

### Step 1: Determine Your Undertone

Hold a white piece of paper next to your face in natural light:
- **Warm undertone.** Your skin looks golden, peachy, or yellowish against the paper.
- **Cool undertone.** Your skin looks pink, rosy, or slightly blue against the paper.
- **Neutral undertone.** No strong lean in either direction.

Most Korean shade systems lean warm to neutral. If you have a strong cool/pink undertone, pay extra attention to shade descriptions.

### Step 2: Match Your Depth

TIRTIR uses numerical shade codes. The general system:
- **11 to 15:** Fair to light
- **17 to 21:** Light to medium
- **23 to 27:** Medium
- **29 to 33:** Medium-deep to tan
- **35 to 45:** Deep

### Step 3: Start With the Mini

[![TIRTIR Mask Fit Red Cushion Mini](/images/products/tirtir-red-mini.jpg)](https://mirai-skin.com/products/tirtir-mask-fit-red-cushion-spf40-pa-4-5g-mini-45shades)

TIRTIR offers mini versions of most cushions. At $10 to $15, they're an affordable way to test your shade before committing to the full-size compact.

## Application Tips

1. **Don't press too hard.** The cushion puff works best with light, bouncing motions (stippling). Pressing hard squeezes out too much product.
2. **Build in thin layers.** One light layer, let it set for 30 seconds, then add more where needed. This prevents cakiness.
3. **Apply sunscreen underneath.** Even cushions with SPF don't provide enough sun protection on their own. Use a [dedicated sunscreen](/best-korean-sunscreens-oily-skin-no-white-cast/) as your base.
4. **Store properly.** Keep the cushion compact closed when not in use. The formula can dry out if left open.
5. **Replace the puff.** The included puff absorbs product and bacteria over time. Replace it monthly or wash it weekly with gentle soap.
