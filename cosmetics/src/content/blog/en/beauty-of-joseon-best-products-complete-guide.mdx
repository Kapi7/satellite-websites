## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Beauty of Joseon cruelty-free?

Yes. Beauty of Joseon is cruelty-free and does not test on animals. Many of their products are also vegan-friendly, though some (like the propolis-based Glow Serum) contain bee-derived ingredients.

### Where is Beauty of Joseon made?

All Beauty of Joseon products are made in South Korea. The brand is based in Seoul and sources many of its hero ingredients — ginseng, rice, propolis — from Korean suppliers.

### Which Beauty of Joseon product should I try first?

Start with the Relief Sun or the Glow Serum. The sunscreen is universally excellent across all skin types, and the Glow Serum delivers the most immediately visible results. Both are under $16.
