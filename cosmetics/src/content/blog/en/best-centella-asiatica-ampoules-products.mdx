Centella asiatica — also called cica, tiger grass, or gotu kola — is the ingredient dermatologists recommend when your skin is angry and everything else makes it worse. It calms inflammation, accelerates wound healing, and strengthens the skin barrier without irritating even the most reactive complexions.

K-beauty figured this out early, and the best centella products still come from Korean brands. Here are six that actually deliver, matched to specific skin concerns.

## Why Centella Works

Centella asiatica contains four active compounds that make it unusually effective:

- **Madecassoside.** The primary anti-inflammatory. Reduces redness and calms irritation at the source.
- **Asiaticoside.** Stimulates collagen synthesis and accelerates wound healing.
- **Madecassic acid.** Supports barrier repair and reduces trans-epidermal water loss.
- **Asiatic acid.** Antioxidant protection that prevents further damage while skin heals.

The combination is why centella works for such a wide range of concerns — redness, acne scars, barrier damage, sensitivity. It's not just soothing the surface; it's supporting actual repair. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can you use centella with retinol?

Yes, and it's one of the best pairings in skincare. Centella calms the irritation and redness that retinol can cause. Apply retinol first, then layer centella on top as a soothing buffer. Many experienced retinol users keep a centella product in their routine permanently for this reason. A gentle starting point is the [COSRX Retinol 0.1 Cream](https://mirai-skin.com/products/cosrx-the-retinol-0-1-cream-20ml) — mild enough that centella easily manages any irritation.

### How long does centella take to work?

For calming acute irritation (redness, stinging), you'll notice a difference within 1-3 applications. For longer-term benefits like scar fading and barrier strengthening, expect 4-8 weeks of consistent daily use.

### Is centella asiatica safe for all skin types?

Yes. Centella is one of the most universally tolerated skincare ingredients. Allergic reactions are exceptionally rare. It's safe during pregnancy, compatible with all other actives, and gentle enough for even eczema-prone skin.
