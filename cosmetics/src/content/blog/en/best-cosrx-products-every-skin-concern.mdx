## For Acne & Breakouts

### BHA Blackhead Power Liquid

> **Chemical exfoliant** · $18 · 100ml · 4% betaine salicylate (equivalent to ~1% salicylic acid)

The K-beauty BHA that converted a generation of acid-skeptics. Betaine salicylate is gentler than pure salicylic acid but still dissolves the sebum plugs that cause blackheads and congestion. Use 2-3 times per week after cleansing, before other treatments.

**What we love:**
- Gentle enough for consistent use without over-exfoliating
- Visibly reduces blackheads within 2-3 weeks
- 100ml bottle lasts 4-6 months at recommended usage

**Best for:** Blackheads, whiteheads, clogged pores, and mild acne. For a primer on choosing your exfoliant, see our [AHA vs BHA vs PHA guide](/aha-vs-bha-vs-pha-which-exfoliant).

---

### Centella Blemish Cream

> **Spot treatment** · $14 · 30ml · Centella asiatica + zinc oxide

Apply directly to active blemishes at night. The centella calms inflammation while zinc dries out the spot. Works best on inflammatory acne (red, swollen pimples) rather than deep cystic acne. See our full [centella products guide](/best-centella-asiatica-ampoules-products) for more from this ingredient.

**What we love:**
- Flattens and calms active pimples overnight
- Prevents post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation from forming
- Small amount goes a long way — one tube lasts months

**Best for:** Active breakouts, inflamed spots, post-acne mark prevention.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is COSRX cruelty-free?

Yes. COSRX is cruelty-free and does not test on animals. The brand is also Leaping Bunny certified. Some products contain animal-derived ingredients (snail mucin, propolis), so they aren't vegan, but no animal testing is involved.

### Where is COSRX made?

All COSRX products are manufactured in South Korea. The brand is headquartered in Seoul and has been producing skincare since 2013.

### What does COSRX stand for?

COSRX stands for "Cosmetics + RX (prescription)." The name reflects the brand's philosophy of creating no-nonsense, treatment-focused skincare that works like a prescription without requiring one.
