## Why K-Beauty Is More Affordable

The Korean beauty market is enormous and intensely competitive. Major brands like COSRX, Beauty of Joseon, and Anua compete on formulation quality rather than luxury branding. They spend less on marketing and packaging and more on ingredients and research.

The result is products that would cost $30 to $50 from a Western brand selling for $10 to $15 at comparable or better quality. This isn't a compromise; it's a different market structure.
