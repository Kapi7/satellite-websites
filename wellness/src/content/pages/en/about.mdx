We started Rooted Glow because we were tired of wellness content that felt either too clinical or too woo-woo. We wanted something in between: practical, grounded, and actually tested by real people.

## What We Believe

Health is not a product you buy. It's built from how you eat, move, sleep, and care for yourself, daily, consistently, without overthinking it. We lean toward ancestral nutrition (real food, minimal processing), functional movement, and skincare routines inspired by Korean beauty traditions.

## Our Editorial Standards

Everything we publish is based on personal experience, peer-reviewed research, or both. We don't accept sponsored posts. When we recommend a product, it's because we've used it ourselves, usually for weeks before writing a word about it.

- No paid placements or sponsored content
- All health claims are backed by cited sources
- Product recommendations come from personal testing
- We update articles when new evidence emerges
