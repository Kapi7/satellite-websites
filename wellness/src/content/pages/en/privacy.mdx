## What We Collect

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-friendly and does not use cookies or track individual users. We see aggregate page views and referrer data. nothing personally identifiable.

## Email

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address solely for sending you our content. You can unsubscribe at any time. We never sell or share your email with third parties.

## Affiliate Disclosure

Some articles on this site may contain affiliate links. This means we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase through those links, at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we genuinely use and believe in.

Affiliate partnerships never influence our editorial opinions. We maintain full independence in what we write and recommend.

## Cookies

This site does not use cookies for tracking. Our analytics solution (Cloudflare Web Analytics) is cookieless by design.
