We started Build Coded because most DIY content online is either too basic ("just screw it together!") or assumes you already have a full workshop. We write for people who want to build real things, whether that's your first shelf or your tenth piece of furniture.

## What We Believe

The best project is the one you finish. We write guides that get you from start to done, with clear steps, realistic tool lists, and honest cost breakdowns. No gatekeeping, no shame about using pocket-hole joinery.

## Our Editorial Standards

Every tool recommendation comes from hands-on testing. Every project has been built at least once by someone on our team. We don't run sponsored content.

- No paid reviews or brand partnerships
- All tools are tested in real workshop conditions
- Project costs include everything, not just the wood
- We update guides when better methods or tools come along
